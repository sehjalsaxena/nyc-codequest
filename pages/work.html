How It Works

How SIFT Works

Discover how SIFT transforms hours of searching into a personalized learning experience in just a few simple steps.

From Confusion to Confidence

Finding the right learning resource shouldn't take longer than learning itself. SIFT understands your goals, analyzes your preferences, and recommends resources that best match your learning style — helping you stop searching and start learning.

The Process

Eight steps, start to finish

Step 01

Create an Account

Sign up securely using Google or Email to save your preferences and learning progress.

Step 02

Complete the Questionnaire

Answer a few simple questions about:

  • Skill to learn
  • Experience level
  • Learning goal
  • Preferred language
  • Learning style
  • Daily study time
Step 03

We Understand Your Profile

Your responses are securely stored and used to build your personalized learning profile.

Step 04

Recommendation Engine

SIFT compares your profile with our curated resource database to identify the most suitable learning resources.

Step 05

Personalized Recommendations

Receive the Top 3 learning resources that best match your goals, experience, and learning preferences.

Step 06

Transparent Explanation

Every recommendation includes a clear explanation describing why it was selected specifically for you.

Step 07

Compare Resources

View side-by-side comparisons, strengths, weaknesses, and understand why alternative resources weren't recommended.

Step 08

Start Learning

Save your recommendation, track your progress, and continue your learning journey from your personalized dashboard.
